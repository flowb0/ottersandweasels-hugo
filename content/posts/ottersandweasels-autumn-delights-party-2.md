# Autumn Delights Party #2
This time our friends Javi & Miguel from upcoming indie noise pop rock sensation [BAITS](https://www.facebook.com/pages/BAITS/819800724726841) are joining us to give you a taste of their musical background.

Resident DJ ottersan is on a musical excursion in Warsaw and won't make it, but no need to cry - we got an even more decent backup:

Andreas S. from theGap music magazine will spin some tracks for you.
