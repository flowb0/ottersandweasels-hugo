# UNA FESTA DI CAFFE'
A fancy small coffee bar has recently opened in Viennas 7th district. Stacked up with a fine selection of beers too this is the perfect place to hang out after a day of hard work. We heard it's Viennas best coffee in town and Mike (the owner) is a really funny host.

We have a DJ gig there to celebrate the official opening and play some crazy music combined with italo flavor.

![](http://i.giphy.com/oaSpoOceHDVe0.gif)

So come and party with us and grab a FRRREEEEEEEE beer
