It's been a while and we got lazy again :)

And we almost forgot about our Best of May and June selection lying around on [Soundcloud](https://soundcloud.com/you/sets).

So here they are, enriched with some very interesting newcomers.

 
### Best of June 2015
From all these great songs I want to take out [I know Leopard](https://iknowleopard.bandcamp.com/) - Close my eyes. I am a total sucker for this 2 voiced singing. "close my eyes, ...." amazing stuff. I can totally picture myself listening to this song and wandering around at the countryside on a cosy warm summer day into the sunset.

Again 2 voices and a very unique lead voice at [Scarlet Band](https://soundcloud.com/jessiescarlet) from UK. I see a big future here (at least in UK).

[Cascine](http://www.cascine.us/) from Stockholm with some crazy ass (but funny) mixture of sounds packed together in some kind of footwork music.

### Best of May 2015
Some driving indie guitar work from [Animal Years](http://www.animalyearsmusic.com/) we like a lot.

Austrian homegrown music from [Kimyan Law](http://www.blumartenmusic.com/artists/kimyan-law/). Already big outside of Austria with his fascinating dreamy beats.

All we are are one of the bands we just can't get enough of. They produce great music and outstanding videos to it.
