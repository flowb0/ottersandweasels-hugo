As part of the Waves Central Europa Music Festival 2015, Mike Skinner showed his new mini documentary produced with Noisey titled [Hip Hop in the holy land](http://noisey.vice.com/noisey-israel-palestine-hip-hop-in-the-holy-land-series/hip-hop-in-the-holy-land-the-godfather-of-palestinian-rap-episode-1). In the documentary Mike Skinner tries to figure out the hip hop scene in Israel and describes what is going on there right now and how the whole scene developed. Here I also have to say that Mike Skinner is one of my personal musical heros, [Original Pirate Material](https://geo.itunes.apple.com/at/album/original-pirate-material/id63836809?at=1010l8cb&mt=1&app=music) is in my Top 10 best albums list of all time.
