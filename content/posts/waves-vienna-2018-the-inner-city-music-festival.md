# Waves Vienna 2018 - back for it's 8th edition
Our beloved inner city showcase music festival it back. Taking place in our home town Vienna for the 8th time. We are excited as every year which artists will surprise us with their stunning music and shows.

This year the focus is on Portugal and Slovakia, so we will see more artists from these two countries on stage.

## The details for the festival
**Date:** 27.-29.9.2018

**Pricing:**

 	- Festival Pass: 47 Euro (or go look in the [facebook fanpage comments](https://www.facebook.com/wavesvienna/), usually there are always people who have to sell their tickets
 	- Conference Pass: 55 Euro
 	- Pro Pass (Festival & Conference): 99 Euro
