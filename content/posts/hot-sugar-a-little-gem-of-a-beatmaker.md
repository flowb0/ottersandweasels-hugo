# Hot Sugar - fresh beats for your ears
I have to admit I am late to the party and just discovered this great artist trough the TV show High Maintenance. But I just fell in love with his music just after listening to a few seconds of his soundscapes. If you like slow mellow dreamy but still punchy beats with this kind of big city vibe in the background then you will also love Hot Sugar.

He is totally into incorporating real world sounds into his music. And that makes it stand out quite a bit.
