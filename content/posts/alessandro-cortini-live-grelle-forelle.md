### If you ask yourself, "who the hell is Alessandro Cortini anyway?":
He is the guy **co-wrote and recorded the Ghosts I­IV with the NINE INCH NAILS** and was performing lead electronics + guitar for Nine Inch Nails and How To Destroy Angels.  Also making music as a solo project under the banner SONOIO (an Italian echo from „sono io“ translating to „it’s me“)

A bit of the "I dream of wires" super cool documentary about modular synths, with the interview of Trent Reznor and Allesandro.
