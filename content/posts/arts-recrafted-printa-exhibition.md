We recommend to check out the exhibition opening of the "arts recrafted" competition in the great concept store and gallery Printa Wien, [Lindengasse 22 (1070 Wien)](https://www.google.at/maps/place/Lindengasse+22,+1070+Wien/@48.2007101,16.3519968,19z/data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x476d078dd33f984f:0x2925fd5e78e98783) on Friday 13th of March, 7 pm.The artworks all deal with the artists' reinterpretation of "Jugendstil" in their contemporary perception. So go there and take the chance to get some drinks with the artists and check out the nice store! The shop offers unique collection of selected design everyday-use items, clothing, eco-friendly accessories and home furnishings with an emphasis on printed and recycled objects. Great place for one of a kind gift. The gallery is dedicated to presenting contemporary serigraphs from upcoming Austrian and international artists.
