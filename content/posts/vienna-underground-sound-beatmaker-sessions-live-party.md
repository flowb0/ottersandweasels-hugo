# Viennas finest underground electronic music live acts
 

On Saturday, the 31st of May you get the chance to witness some raw electronic music talent at Celeste.

The Beatmaker Sessions are going LIVE and on top of the monthly club at Fluc there will be a big party with amazing Live acts. Therefore they picked some skilled electronic musicians who will put their talent on display and work their analog machines, synths and midi controllers. If you wanna know what's going on in the (real) viennese electronic music scene you definitely have to check them out.
