For 3 years now the Beatmaker Sessios by [Sm:artguns](http://smartguns.eu/) is hosting shows and releasing compilations with the best of Austrian underground music. [
](http://smartguns.eu)

The main focus is to feature unknown Beatmakers and Artists and give them a chance to play live and connect with other electronic music lovers.

Also this year the Beatmaker sessions compilation is a stunning glimpse into the Austrian underground scene of electronic music artists.

See here the [Full List of selected Artists](http://beatmakersessions.eu/beatmaker-sessions-compilaton-vol-3-artists/) for the Volume 3 of the Compilation Series.

The Party to celebrate the release takes place at Celeste on Friday the 19th of December.

with Levni, Monostikz, Mosch and TSI playing LIVE
