Slowly the sun is fighting its way back and brings us nice weather here in Vienna.

April Selection is out. Diverse as the weather this month, but full of excitement for the upcoming summer :)

We've got [Jack White](http://jackwhiteiii.com/) with the single to his upcoming album. [Twin Shadow](http://twinshadow.net/) is also back in the game and stronger than ever before. Can't wait for the record. Strangely everybody is going for the hats now. Pharrell, Twin Shadow, .... [Chet Faker](http://chetfaker.com/) is omnipresent in the last few months and totally deserves it. If you haven't heard of him yet check also out [his amazing boiler room live set in Melbourne](http://boilerroom.tv/recording/chet-faker/).

[The Kooks](http://thekooks.com/) will also release there new record soon and put out their single "Around Town" which we like quite a bit. So we expect something big here.

If you are in the mood for some beautiful atmospheric piano music, check out [Nils Frahm](http://www.nilsfrahm.com/). We had the chance to watch him 2013 at Primavera Sound Festival and it was just mind blowing. Check out his (https://vimeo.com/78130481).

So let the outside season begin, enjoy our soundtrack for ping-pong-tournaments and lazy sun-days!
