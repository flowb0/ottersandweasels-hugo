There is a cool Festival during the next week from today 8th of June till 13th of June 2015 in Vienna called** [Unsafe+Sounds Festival](http://unsafeandsounds.com/)**. Exciting underground music and exhibitions.

Here is some music from the curators, so you know what to expect :)
