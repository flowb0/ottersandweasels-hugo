# The Popfest 2018 brings a ton of fresh Austrian music
In it's 9th year we are really looking forward what the curators Nino Mandl and Katharina Seidler will present us in a 3-day series of free concerts of the finest Austrian bands out there.

The Popfest starts Thursday 26th of July and takes places mainly at the Karlsplatz area with the main stage right in front of the Karlskirche.
