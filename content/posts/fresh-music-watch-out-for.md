We got some fresh music promos for you. Watch out for these Artists:

1) The new NYC/LA-based Youngbloods label will release the debut from Vermont producer **Sasquatch**. It's a unique take on house music I really like.
On XLRT they have one track already free to listen, [check it out](http://www.xlr8r.com/mp3/2014/10/when-it-rains-boxwork-remix). The artwork and design of the Youngbloods is also quite cool. I am looking forward to their next releases.
Sasquatch Ep will be out 11th November.

2) Austrian Beat Wizard **abby lee tee** is back with a remix of Ritornell - Aquarium Eyes. It's deep and watery with some fancy drumwork. Listen and Download [here](https://soundcloud.com/abbyleetee/ritornell-aquarium-eyes-abby-lee-tee-remix).

3) Some good pop music from Swedish singer/songwriter **Charlotte Eriksson**. A cool animated drawings video was made for her first single of the new record.

{{< youtube "x56C325GJdo" >}}

4) Somehow more pop music but in a more sophisticated dark way by **Tom Bright**. He's got a really interesting voice, check his new track "Berlin" out.

 

5) And some dirty bluesy rock'n'roll by **Ziegler Co. **Listen to their track [The Recluse (from the EP 'Portraiture') here.](https://soundcloud.com/foof-records/ziegler-co-the-recluse-from-the-ep-portraiture) Also a lot of potential here.
