## If you look for a nice present for your music loving friends, Sharks in space is the right place for you.
It is an Austrian start-up which brings you fresh music from Austrian (underground) artists delivered in a stylish cardboard box every month. They focus especially on instrumental and experimental styles plus electronica, techno, drone, ambient and sound art :)

And NEWS NEWS NEWS, from now on their package will contain VINYL (Hell Yeah) instead of CDs and sometimes also international artists.

We really like this approach. This is how a box looks like.
