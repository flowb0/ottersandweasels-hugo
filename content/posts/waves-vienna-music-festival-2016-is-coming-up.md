Like every year, for the last 3 years we give you the heads up for the upcoming Waves Vienna Music Festival 2016.

This years motto is „East meets West“, so numerous international alternative, electronic, rock, and club acts will be performing around Vienna’s 9th district. Additionally, local artists and sophisticated musicians from Eastern Europe form an essential part of the festival’s programme.

It has shrunk a bit but nevertheless the quality of music should be very good as usual.

The good part of making a festival a bit smaller is that also the ticket price decreases :)

So now you can watch a ton of concerts from 2[9.SEP.-1.OCT.2016 for just 56€](http://www.wavescentraleurope.com/tickets-3) in Vienna, which is quite nice.

Also the venues are now super close together and all in walking distance in the 9th district, which is also a big advantage to the last year.
