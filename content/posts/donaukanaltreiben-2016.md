For the 10th year now the Donaukanaltreiben takes place at the Viennese Donaukanal.

Today it starts (27.05) and continues till Sunday 29th May.

Various Bands (from Austria) will perform, DJs will DJ and lots of other stuff is going on. BUT only till 22:00 outside. Then the Clubs close to Donaukanal like Flex, dasWerk and Grelle Forelle will continue with the party. All the concerts and performances outside (at the Donaukanal) will be free.
## What you need to know about the Donaukanaltreiben 2016
