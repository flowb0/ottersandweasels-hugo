We are on the hunt for fresh music. Especially in Spring, when everything starts to blossom, we also awake from our winter hiatus :)

A first discovery is
## [Whitney](http://www.whitneytheband.com/)
from Chicago. They consist of two guitars, bass, keys, horns and drums and play kind of melancholic Pavement style music. The Album should be out in Spring (so about nowish) on [Secretly Canadian](http://www.secretlycanadian.com/), a label we really like.
