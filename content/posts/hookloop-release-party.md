## Have some fresh Austrian music:
Our inbox told us that there is a wonderful concert at [B72](http://www.b72.at/) this Friday **the 26th of September**. [Hook+Loop](http://www.hookandloop.at/) will release their first album and therefore they will present it to the world. I guess it will be quite exciting to hear Violin, Cello, Piano and Drums at this stage. They mix Jazz with Pop, Rock and new music so be prepared for a night of delightful pop music sound in a new interpretation. Press text says: "

hook+loop add to the musical landscape with their very own mix of pop, rock, jazz and new

music, all the while challenging genre boundaries and breaking conventions."
