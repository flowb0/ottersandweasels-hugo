Hi there,

It's been a while that we made some recommendations for concerts. But here is one.

[Svavar Knutur](http://www.svavarknutur.com/) the icelandic troubadour and singer-songwriter with the exceptional smooth voice is coming to Vienna.

He is playing Tuesday 09th of February 2016 at [Clash](http://www.bandsintown.com/event/10643598-svavar-knutur-vienna-clash-2016?artist=Svavar+Knutur), Fluchtgasse 9, 1090 Wien. It says 8:00 pm. And I guess it will be pay as you wish.

I had the honor to be his stage manager at [Waves Vienna Music Festival 2011 at Dogenhof.](http://www.yelp.at/biz_photos/waves-vienna-festival-wien?select=diQ-1oFO5xh7b1K9TI7tOQ) This guy blew everybody away. He was by far the best act I had on all 3 days on my stage, the Dogenhof. I bought 2 CDs right after the concert, but over the years they got lost somewhere, hehe. But I am really glad to see this great musician again in Vienna.

And he was also hilarious. You get a great concert plus some good laughs. Like almost all scandinavians he knows a bit of german and uses it in a very charming way.
