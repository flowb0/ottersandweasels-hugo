Hey peepz, we are proud to announce that we will play some music at the **Slow Tacos** Pop up Restaurant at Donaukanal.

Our Slow Tacos DJ Sets will be starting at 18:00 till 23:00.

**And we are playing Friday 12th of June and Thursday 9th of July.**

The restaurant is an idea of[ the Culinary Love Band Project](http://www.tclb.at/locations/slow-tacos/)  and brings you, quote "mind blowing tacos freshly made from locally sourced ingredients, tank beer, craft beer and oh-so-good cocktails." So we can't wait to try it ourselves.

![](http://i.giphy.com/fvLTnKMAhp1hm.gif)

You can check out [the menu here](http://www.tclb.at/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/ST_Menu_FB_150519_small.pdf).

In the last two years they already brought us the Big Smoke (2014) and It's all about the meat (2013) pop up restaurants to Vienna.

The adress is  Franz-Josefs-Kai 3, 1010 Vienna, Austria, right next to Badeschiff at Donaukanal.

And don't forget they don't take reservations. It's first come, first served policy.

We will play some new stuff mixed with all our old favorites of the (indie)pop, funk, sould, r'n'b, hip-hop universe, flavored with some mariachi sounds.
