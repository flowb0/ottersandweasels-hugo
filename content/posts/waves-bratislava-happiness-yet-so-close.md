Finally this year we made it to the third edition of the [WAVES Bratislava](http://www.wavescentraleurope.com/category/wavesbratislava-en/) and still we were sad we only made it on the last night, because it was the bomb!

Via bus we went from the WAVES Vienna's festival centre at Alte Post to the Bratislava city centre. The bus only takes an hour and leaves each hour in both directions. With the festival pass for both cities the ride is for free.

clubs (LINKS!!!)

atmosphere (BILDER!!!)

old market hall - people dancing and jumping

everything very close together in 5 minutes you can reach all the venues

prices are very cheap and therefore a lot of people can access the concerts, for sure also adjusted to the slovakian prices but for Austrian visitors like us - heaven!

The venues were very delicate and had a very delicate touch.
