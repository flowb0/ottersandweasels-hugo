creativity is what matters, this inspiring movie features contemporary artists from all kind of fields from James Franco to Jonas Mekas. Short interview with composers, filmmakers, curators, photographers, painters tell about their main drivers for artistic work. It's never the money but always the process of creation is the only choice. The inevitable need to produce something out of yourself, inspired by other humans, love or whatever is what makes art so full and rich. "you never do it for the money - creative processes" is a stunning mini documentary about this the creative process.
