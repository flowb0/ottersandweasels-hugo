After watching the first rehearsal live at the Stadthalle today with all contestants for the Finals, Belgium is still my favorite. Seeing their light show live is quite impressive compared to it on TV. I read that Loic also had some influence on the visuals and helped design them plus he wrote the song.

Some other contestants shows/performances appear also quite different than on TV. Latvia sounded not as good as on TV. Spain was kind of 2 times louder than every other country but worked quite good. I think they also have a good chance to win this thing. The crowd went pretty crazy for Serbia when the beat kicked in :) and Sweden's show looks also better on TV than live at the venue.
