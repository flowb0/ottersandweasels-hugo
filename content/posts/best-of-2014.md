# And here it is, our best of selection for 2014.
![](http://media.giphy.com/media/TZIHB9UNIXBO8/giphy.gif)

Some fine pop tunes mixed with fresh R'n'B and Hip-Hop heros popping up from the underground. Of course a few indie-rock tracks also made it on the list, but as electronic music and hip-hop is getting stronger every year, this development is also visible in our best of 2014 list. Enjoy :)

Watch out especially for [Rae Sremmurd](https://soundcloud.com/raesremmurd), they will be huge in 2015.
