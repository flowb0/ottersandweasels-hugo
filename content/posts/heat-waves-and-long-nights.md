As this summer turned out to be tropical and really worthwhile, also the nights turned out longer lasting than usually. When the average temperature in your apartment rises over 30°C you better exchange the city for cooler country live (as we did for a week in the middle of July). But if you can't and won't flee the big city life there are a lot of opportunities to cool oneself down and enjoy the summer as much as possible. My last week pretty much had all these hot summer nights happenings inside, so take a look and check what you could add to your and especially to my list:

(- starting the week by getting up early as the heat didn't allow me more than 4-5 hours of sleep, taking the time to sort our old clothes, stuff I didn't use within the last 2 years and moved from it from flat to flat, making myself heavier as necessary. As there is an urgent need for things I rearranged my living space and donated nice and useful things that could definitely make someone else happy too (link caritas wirhelfen). I gave a way my 2nd sleeping bar, 2nd hammock, 2nd pair of running shoes, as these are things I already purchased two times, or got as presents and I can only use 1 sleeping bag a night and I know people who really need it right now. )
