Strolling around trough the interweb I found these guys. I thought 3 is a good number of music acts to remember, so here is our fresh music selection:

 
## Manor
From Australia, still pretty small, but with a very nice dream pop indie shoegaze vibe and their debut EP called 'They've Come Into My Home'.

Which, I guess, will make them a bit more famous. Hypemachine already picked them up. So if you like Tame Impala you will also like these guys.

{{< youtube "8EYkmD0cggQ" >}}

 

 
## [Denzel Curry](https://soundcloud.com/denzelcurryph)
Massive Trappy (Gangsta) Hip-Hop from Florida. Bring out the lean peeeeepzzzz :) I love his productions, perfect dark Synth lines + super heavy Trap (Cloud) beats.

Fact Magazine made a very cool mini Documentary. check it out.

{{< youtube "D2Z9_CUSUL4" >}}

**WHY is he not at the Hip-Hop Open in Wiesen ???????, I repeat, WHYYYYYYY**

{{< youtube "pCecBzMpdpw" >}}

but at least they got [Oddisee](https://www.ottersandweasels.com/oddisee-the-good-company-live-waves-2015/) at the Hip-Hop open this year. So maybe next year, don't forget about Denzel Curry.

 

 
## [Yoke Lore](https://soundcloud.com/yokelore)
Also already picket up by Hypemachine, or just promoted to be picked up by Hypemachine? You never know. But anyway, good stuff by Adrian Galvin from Brooklyn.

Dreamy synth (indie) pop, with a voice to remember, and overall very good vocal work and top notch production skillzzz.

{{< youtube "2IL5wbaoQHY" >}}

The video is a bit strange, but hey, artistic freedom and stuff. The debut EP *Far Shore *should be out already.
