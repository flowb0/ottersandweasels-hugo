Some have noticed, some didn't, but there is this huge once in a lifetime thing going on Saturday 20th of September in Krakow - Poland. One of the best electronica labels ever, Warp records, is celebrating its 25th birthday with a huge party. Literally 30 seconds after I have seen the lineup I bought my ticket. Didn't really check how far or close Krakow is from Vienna, but who cares if you can see Autechre, Battles, Hudson Mohawke, Rustie, Darkstar, Patton ... for about 30€. Plus the location is some abandoned old Soviet Hotel - Forum Hotel.

[![Warp_25_Krakow_Poster_2_750_375_90_s](/images/uploads/2014/09/Warp_25_Krakow_Poster_2_750_375_90_s.jpg)](https://www.ottersandweasels.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/09/Warp_25_Krakow_Poster_2_750_375_90_s.jpg)
