# Penguin Prison is back - Hooray
We discovered [Penguin Prison](http://penguinprison.com/) last year with his amazing track Calling out.  Chris Glover aka Penguin Prison is playing electropop (but the good one) and is from New York. He just released his new record "Lost in New York" and we like this 80s synth pop stuff a lot :) He is a kick-ass songwriter and producer. So check it out here.
