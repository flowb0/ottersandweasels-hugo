## adding some flavor - female rapper SZA from Top Dawg Entertainment
female rappers are still rarely seen and if they have to fight twice as hard in the world of pure testosterone and man-power - HIPHOP. But the women who make and stay in our heads, like [Missy Elliott](http://www.missy-elliott.com/), [Aaliyah](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aaliyah) and M.I.A. and it seems now we found a new one: [SZA](http://www.iamsza.com/)

She's the first lady on Kendrick Lamar's [TDE](http://www.txdxe.com/) label!! She released her first album last month.

The first single "warm winds" brings nice sounds to our ears and offer a glance at the potential lying in SZA's voice and make you want more, instantly. The male vocals come from [Isaiah Rashad](https://soundcloud.com/isaiah_rashad).

And in addition to having a great song this video really has something. With the looks like being taped in the 80ies - early 90ies and a landscape that could be almost everywhere, very much reminded me of the area I grew up and a perfect play between slow-motion, flickering pictures and giving speed at the right point.

We really like it and hope for more to come!

And if you're interested in some outfit-inspirations: [SZA's instagram](http://instagram.com/justsza) is full of awesome outfits!
