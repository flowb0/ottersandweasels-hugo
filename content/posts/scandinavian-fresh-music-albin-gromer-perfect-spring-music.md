# Albin Gromer delivers perfect spring music with his indie soul funk easy listening pop
I have to admin I just discovered this little gem :) But I am totally in love with this music. Must be the spring time too.

Check out his amazing album from this swedish wunderkind called "Album"  (I am not kidding) from 2012. I even like it more than the new one from 2017 [called "Narren"](https://open.spotify.com/album/2Y1pn7NZnYSSsl2jR4lEor?si=Rp7eOqwkQX-kwFQu2JwHJA).
