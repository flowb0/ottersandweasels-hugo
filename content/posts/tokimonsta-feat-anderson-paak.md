[TOKiMONSTA](http://www.tokimonsta.com/) released the video for his new track "Put It Down" yesterday. The song features [Anderson .Paak](http://www.andersonpaak.com/) & [KRNE](https://soundcloud.com/krane). The song has this addictive quality to it that you wanna listen to it over and over again and the video just satisfies this as well by the stunts Anderson .Paak does (in the long black leather coat) and sexy women playing with glasses and knives. The Matrix like look of Anderson .Paak ket in a grey to black atmosphere that is in marked contrast to the warmish red scenes with the women and an old guy. Good work. Just watch below.
