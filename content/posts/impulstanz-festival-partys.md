It's that time of the year again :)

Impulstanz Festival is in town and if you read this and have never been to a Vestibül Impulstanz Party

![](http://assets.noisey.com/content-images/contentimage/35518/Bildschirmfoto-2014-07-17-um-16-18-58.jpg)

, just pack your things and go there.

For us it's the best party time of the year in Vienna. The Vestibül has the perfect size to make everybody dance and of course it doesn't hurt that some proper impulstanz dancers are always on the floor, haha.
