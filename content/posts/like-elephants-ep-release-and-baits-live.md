We have a recommendation for you cause one of our favorite Labels in Vienna is hosting a very nice party on Wednesday 13th of May at [Rhiz](http://rhiz.org/) (Vienna).

[Like Elephants](https://www.facebook.com/likeelephants) is one of the newest signings to [Noiseappeal Records](http://www.noiseappeal.com/index.php?id=3&no_cache=1) and they will play live and present their new 7" EP "Home". Their music can be described as Indie/Dream-Pop and I can't wait to see them live on stage for the first time.
