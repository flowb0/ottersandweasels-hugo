# Big Party to celebrate Art's Birthday at ORF-RadioKulturhaus Vienna today: 17th of January
AND IT'S FREE ENTRY . That would be the most important information.

A french guy called [Robert Filliou](http://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Filliou) (1926–1987) invented [this event](http://www.artsbirthday.net/) and we thank him for that. Art's Birthday is a global thing and the Austrian part will be hosted by the notorious [Rokko](http://www.rokkosadventures.at/)(who is publishing the best art&music magazine in Austria) and Austrian Radio Station Ö1. The party takes places in a very special location: the ORF Funkhaus, at Argentinierstrasse 30, 1040 Wien. It starts at 20:00.

![rokkos artsbirthday party](http://www.rokkosadventures.at/bilder/artsbirthday_2014_flyer.jpg )

So be there or be square.

If it's too crazy you can still check out Sexy Deutsch. We hope it is better than the last few times. The started out very strong, but got worse and worse. But still, theoretically there should be some good music.
