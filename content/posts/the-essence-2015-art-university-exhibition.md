From Thursday 25th of June till Sunday the Art University in Vienna ("Angewandte") is showcasing the work of the students at their art exhibition "The essence 2015" at Künstlerhaus. Thursday will be free entry and a big party.

Might be one of the best partys of the year. So we advised you to check it out.
