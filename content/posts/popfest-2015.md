For the 6th time the Popfest 2015 is taking place around the Karlsplatz Area in Vienna. From 23-26th of July you can watch numerous pretty good Austrian Artists for free. The main attraction, if there is good weather, is the "Seebühne" stage in front of Karlskirche built into the pond.

 Bild: © Simon Brugner theyshootmusic.com
