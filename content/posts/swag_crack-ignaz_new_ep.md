One of the best yet, [Crack Ignaz](https://www.facebook.com/IgnazK) is back with a new mixtape called [Ewige Nacht 500000](https://crackignaz.bandcamp.com/album/ewige-nacht-500000). And it's fucking great. So DOWNLOAD IT IMMEDIATELY

It's finest austrian rap with a certain twist, a funky weirdness and deep (sometimes funny) lyrics. The production is tight, the 808s are booming and the Hi-Hats are sharp as knives. We are really looking forward to the next video for the EP.

SWAG SWAG SWAG is our favorite track from the EP so far with these asian/japanese sounding synths/samples. Narco Polo is even going in a gangsta rap direction. Quite impressive :)

We are still wondering why he isn't already huge in Germany, possibly because they don't understand a thing he is rapping, haha.

Keep up the good work. We LOVE IT.
