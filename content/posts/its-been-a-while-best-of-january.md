It's been a while since our last "best of-list", but a lot happened in the ottersandweasels den. Right now I don't wanna tell too much, but you will hear more soon.
Anyways now to our Best of January List. Featuring our favorite tracks of the month and new stuff we discovered that we wanna share with you.
But due to the long absence of this category we also added some of our favorite tracks released already in late 2015.
One of the biggest surprises is the diversity our music list is seeing these days, with artists from all around the world and all different kind of genres. The Russian artist [Kedr Livanskiy](https://soundcloud.com/kedr_livanskiy) for instance is one of those nice surprises that got my heart beating faster and accompanied me on my walks through the not winter not spring Vienna. She definitely managed to cheer me up and blow away my blues.
The french electro pop artist [Yanis ](https://soundcloud.com/mynameisyanis)makes dancers dance hypnotized to his beats in the video for his song "Hypnotized".
[Young Thug](https://soundcloud.com/youngthugworld) makes a clear statement in his new song "F cancer" and it's as loud and dirty as it needs to be to really fight it.
[Sioux Falls](https://soundcloud.com/sioux-falls) from Oregon bring beach feeling into our living room with their song "dinosaur dying". A classic indie rock song with awesome lyrics. [Tinashe's](https://soundcloud.com/tinashenow) new song "Ride of your life" is just damn cool. And my personal "Ohrwurm" of the month is [Jeremih's](http://www.jeremih.com/) "Pass dat".

Have fun with our selection and enjoy!
