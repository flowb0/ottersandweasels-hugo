Soon it will be here! The long awaited second EP by the Viennese Dream Pop wunderwuzi [Sleep Sleep](http://www.sleepsleep.org/). As he did before with the [CVRS 1 EP ](https://noiseappealrecords.bandcamp.com/album/cvrs-digital-ep)he takes a bunch of his favorite songs and remodels them completely into the signature Sleep Sleep sound.

The EP will be released step by step (or in May?) and we can't wait to here more after listening over and over to the first single "Just Kids" (Sex Jams Cover) which is already here with a very cool oldschool DIY video.
