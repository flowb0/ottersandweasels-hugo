One of our favorite bands And so I watch you from afar is back in Vienna tonight (22nd of May, 2015). As we wanted to visit Ljubljana for quite a while now and it's just a few hours away from Vienna, we hopped on the train and already watched them a few days ago there at the [Kino Siska Venue](http://www.kinosiska.si/en). It was amazing how tight they play and it is hard for me to imagine if they can get any better. These 2 voiced guitar solos are just always in time and it is just pure joy to watch them play live. Some people are complaining about the fact that there is a bit more singing on the new record "Heirs", but in my opinion the singing adds just another cool facett to the already amazing post rock sound collage ASIWYFA are producing.
