# Movie premiere + Unplugged Session with Kim Taylor
 

At the [Stadtkino Wien ](http://stadtkinowien.at/) "I Used to Be Darker" will premiere on 10th of January. More cinemas showing the movie are: Moviemento Linz, Graz KIZ, Pro­grammkino Wels .

![I used to be darker at Stadtkino Vienna](https://pbs.twimg.com/media/Bb7_It-CcAA7vDC.jpg:large) I used to be darker at Stadtkino Vienna

[IMDB ](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt2147728/)says: "A runaway seeks refuge with her aunt and uncle in Baltimore and finds their marriage ending and her cousin in crisis. In the days that follow, the family struggles to let go of the past while searching for new things to hold onto."

A bit depressing I guess and the rating is just 6.1, but the upside is the movie made it to the [Sundance Film Festival](http://www.sundance.org/festival/) and right after the premiere in Vienna, actress and Singer/Songwriter [Kim Taylor](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hvQCA5VDrz4) will be playing an unplugged show.
