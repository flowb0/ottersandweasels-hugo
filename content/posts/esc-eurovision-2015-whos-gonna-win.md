The Eurovision Song Contest is in town and just 5 more days to the big final on Saturday. We could manage to get one press accreditation and will try to catch some pics of the atmosphere of this whole huge event for you. The Press Centre is quite impressive and has enough space for the ~1500 journalists and bloggers from all over the world. It's a buzzing atmosphere in there, especially when the big screen is on and showing a rehearsal of one of the contestants from the nearby hall. For the main event the press is not allowed to see the Live show (bummer).

But still it will be an exciting evening and maybe a close call:

![](http://i.giphy.com/XAlKNg0sq9D2g.gif)

A lot of bidding is already going on who's gonna win this year and to make it easier for you we have a nice compilation for you lined up:
## The Contestants of the ESC2015
Since a few years the the Big 5 have always a fixed starting position in the main event (cause they pay the most money to the EBU Organization, which produces the Event) and the winner of the last year (=Austria). All the other countries have to compete in a semifinal show to get voted to the final show.
