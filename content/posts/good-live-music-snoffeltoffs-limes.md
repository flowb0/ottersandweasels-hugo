This weekend the Austrian record label Noise Appeal Records is hosting another concert session with amazing bands for a bargain.

Expect some great music.

**Saturday, the 19th of July** the German Lo-Fi-Garage heroes from **SNOFFELTOFFS will play at DasBach supported by LIMES from Austria**.
