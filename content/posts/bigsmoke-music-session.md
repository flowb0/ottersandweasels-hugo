# BigSmoke OttersAndWeasels Session
Great Food+Great Music = Big Smoke Ottersandweasels Session. On** Monday the 8th of September** we present a Best of ranging from Indie-Pop, R'n'B, Hip-Hop, Soul, Funk and so on**[ at the BigSmoke BBQ Pop Up Restaurant at Donaukanal Vienna](http://www.bigsmoke.at/).** Hang out at the Bar and have some Kozel or join us for some tasty BBQ and listen to an equally tasteful music selection.

During the last months we have been a bit lazy with putting together fresh playlists but today we will play a five hour best-of Selection from our music knowledge. If some mp3s got lost during the years on our harddisks we will try to use the almighty power of the internet to bring these ones back to life via highly professional 3G streaming - iPhone - Aux Cable - Mixer connection :)
