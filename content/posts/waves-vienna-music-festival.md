It's that time of the year again. Waves Vienna Music Festival & Conference is right around the corner. From 2nd till 4th of October Viennas finest clubs and concert venues will be hosting artist from all over the world. Since last year the Waves Festival is also taking place at various locations in Bratislava. The perfect chance to have cheap drinks and check out the coolest clubs. This years focus countries of the showcase festival are Croatia and the Netherlands. The thing we like most about this festival is that you can easily walk from location to location cause most of the venues are in the 2nd district of Vienna. Plus you can always discover a lot of hidden talent there with all this artists on the rise. For all the music industry professionals and people interested in the music industry there is also a conference part with pretty amazing speakers, like [Peter Jenner](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_Jenner) (former manager of Pink Floyd) and interesting topics like "Can Musicians Still Make Money with Recordings?". For the whole conference program click here.
