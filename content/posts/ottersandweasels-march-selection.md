Yes Yes Yo, We don't stop, Ottersandweasels rock on !!! Who know's this one?

I guess it's easier when you have KRS One rapping it in your head, haha.

Anyway, March Selection is out. We searched in the immense depths of the internet to find some special tracks for you out there.

So here we are with Wild Beasts, Pharrell Williams, Wu-Tang and many more combined in an eclectic mix. By the way you should also read about Wu-Tangs plan to [just release one copy of their new record for 5.000.000 $](https://music.yahoo.com/blogs/music-news/wanna-buy-wu-tang-clan-album-221351436.html). Some new strategy for music distribution ahead, maybe they will change the game (at least a bit).

AAANNNNDDD one of our favorite acts is back with a pretty strong vital sign. [FEW NOLDER ](https://soundcloud.com/fewnolder)released a 2 minutes snippet of a new track which got us really excited. We had the chance to meet him in 2010 at the first Klub Kariert Party and there he also played a hell of a live-set. So club promoters out there, I advise you strongly to get this guy back to Austria for a gig :)
