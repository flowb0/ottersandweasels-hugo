We are a bit late this month but the weather was just too nice so we had to hang out a lot in the sun.

This month we changed the game a little bit and put in our May selection more songs from artists we saw last month play live in Vienna.

A fresh artist we totally spaced out is[ FKA Twigs](http://wwwater.me/). The beats and this voice in combination is just amazing. So better late than never discovered by us :)

[Kraftwerk](http://www.kraftwerk.com/) did their show marathon at Burgtheater, which was supposed to be every show a different album, but in the end it was somehow just a few songs from a specific album and then always the same show. A lot of people were quite pissed off because they got a few tickets for different shows and ended up seeing more or less the same show a few times.

The[ Beatmaker Sessions](http://beatmakersessions.eu/) made a big party and one of the outstanding artists playing there were[ Levni & Sloth Pallas](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XAgVEBgxZVE). We just had to put their hymn like song "Tandur" in the may list after watching them playing a perfect show at Beatmaker Sessions Party.

So enjoy the music and keep on rockin !
