# 18 Austrian Artists at Eurosonic 2014
Yes Yes Yes. This years focus at the Eurosonic Noorderslag 2014 is on Austria. So 18 Artists have been chosen by the [Austrian Music Export](http://www.musicexport.at/) Organization to play there live at the biggest showcase festival in Europe from 15th – 18th of January 2014 in Groningen (Netherlands). A few of our favorites are also there like [Hella Komet](http://hellacomet.bandcamp.com/album/wild-honey) and [Fuckhead](http://noiseappealrecords.bandcamp.com/album/avoid-nil-digital-ep) from [Noise Appeal Records](http://www.noiseappeal.com/index.php?id=3&no_cache=1). So if you are somewhere close, go check them out.

If you wanna know more about the festival, [MICA](http://www.musicaustria.at/en/magazine/pop-rock-electronic/austria-focus-country-eurosonic-noorderslag-2014) has a very nice article about it. And for german speaking people [FM4](http://fm4.orf.at/stories/1726039/), [theGap](http://www.thegap.at/musikstories/artikel/eurosonic-2014-all-eyes-on-austria/) and [derStandard](http://derstandard.at/1381374126114/Showcase-Festival-Eurosonic-mit-Oesterreich-Schwerpunkt) wrote also about it.
