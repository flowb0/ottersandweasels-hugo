# 25 year anniversary of the Sziget Festival is coming up in August 2017
Crazy that this amazing festival already exists for 25 years :) I was there about 10 times and lost count over the years. And still it is my favorite festival in whole Europe.

You got super big bands, super small bands and everything in between and can party the whole night and the whole week through.

It is taking place in the middle of Hungarys capital Budapest at the danube island from 09th of August till 15th of August = 6 days of festival madness.

Here is a cool map of the festival from 2016 so you get a better impression how huge it is.

Even more info about accommodation, the line up, how to travel there and much much more you find on the [official Sziget Festival website](http://en.szigetfestival.com/).

 

![map 2016 sziget](/images/uploads/2017/04/map-2016-sziget.png)

So the big advantage is that you can easily stay in a hotel or hostel or Air B'n'B in the city and go to the festival every day.
