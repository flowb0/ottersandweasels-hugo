# Watch Anderson .Paak joins Gospel Choir in “Come Down” Cover
## As a prelude to his Grammy night performance with A Tribe Called Quest.

{{< youtube "2P-9DVSbiF4" >}}

"Come Down" is one of my favorite songs on [Anderson .Paak's ](http://www.andersonpaak.com/)latest album [Malibu](http://radi.al/MalibuiTunes). As it all started last August when I discovered the video from one of their live gigs for NPR Music. Since amazing Anderson .Paak and his alike great band The Free Nationals convey this unique groove that just makes you tap your toes and the whole room is filled with this special "ooooohhhhh".

And being one of my favorite artists in 2016 the love goes on with the latest video for the song "Come Down" released February 8 as a perfect preview of this years [Grammys](https://www.grammy.com/) on Sunday.

The drummer and singer is nominated for both Best New Artist and Best Urban Contemporary Album.

According to the description, the video features many of .Paak’s friends and family from his hometown of Oxnard, CA. Both his sister Camille and his 6-year-old son Soul make appearances.

The Grammys ceremony, which includes a performance from .Paak, will broadcast live from LA’s Staples Center at 8pm (EST) on February 12 via CBS.
