As we just returned from vacation we just got to see some stuff on Saturday from the Popfest and somehow also just made it into the Brut. So our Popfest 2015 review is quite short.

But why repeat something if some good articles are already out there -> Walkerkönige Pop Fest review ["Popfest 2015 Awards"](http://walzerkoenig.tumblr.com/post/125179178919/popfest-2015-awards-viennas-popfest-has-a)

We were just waiting more or less for [Crack Ignaz ](https://crackignaz.bandcamp.com)

and even after 1 hour delay we couldn't wipe that grin from our face when he started with one of our favorite Tracks SWAG SWAG SWAG :)

{{< youtube "kpGwVSyInTA" >}}

And I have to admit I didn't like what Wandl was doing up there. Somehow his mic was even louder than the mic of the main MC?????, so I wasn't sure what was going on there (who the hell was doing the Sound, haha). I have never heard that before that the Hype Man/ Backup Mc / DJ is louder than the main act / MC. And come on, the pitching down of the voice is funny for a few lines but the whole concert ???

Anyway, enough with the ranting, it's just my opinion, we also filmed (super professional iphone quality) a bit of "Herbert Prohaska" to capture the good vibes :)

{{< youtube "_5h1nmbwofg" >}}

We caught about 15 minutes of Sam Irl at Roxy which was quite cool, but I guess absolutely nobody got what he was really doing, cause this guy is a genius but the audience just thinks he is "djing".

And we missed more or less everything else from the Popfest 2015 but what can you do.
