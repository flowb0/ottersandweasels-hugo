# Two very promising indie bands are playing on Tuesday at DasBach (->Facebook Event)

[VAGUE](https://soundcloud.com/vague1000):

for the feeling and the transcendent mood. Ethereal indie pop riding waves of reverb.

and  

[ELLIS](http://ellis.bandcamp.com/): -> [YouTube Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=H0U5-gXK__k)

noisy melancholy from Vienna. Melody meets distortion.

Since [dasBach](http://dasbach.at/) got a new Soundsystem and a supertalented Sound engineer it is the best live music venue in Vienna for a 100-150 pax concert (IMHO).

Plus it is** just 3€ entry** for two upcoming bands. So there is absolutely no excuse not to watch this concert :)

Join us for a nice concert evening at DasBach and check out their new soundsystem.

Das Bach. 13.05

start: 21:00 h

entry fee:  3 Euros
