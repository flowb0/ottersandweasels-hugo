# why ottersandweasels - just because they can
Hi there! We are a bunch of people from Vienna who love music, and this is our blog.
The OttersandWeasels music blog was founded in 2013 and does all kinds of awesome things for the Viennese community and the Austrian music scene.
It's about catchin the vibe of a city our home-/playground, vienna and of course Austria. Whatever you wanna call it, we love music, and wanna share this with you, getting feedback about it, talking about music, already filling our days and evenings since we are teenagers, contributing to numerous bands, or having had a traditional education in classical music, growing up in the 80ies and 90ies transforms our tastes into hybrid sculptures embracing Stevie Wonder, Aaliyah, Michael Jackson, Beastie Boys, Kyuss,  Boards of Canada, .... and whatever attracts our ears. So stay tuned for some wonderful music.

We’re always on the look out for new music addicts who can write about their findings in an entertaining and informative way.
 
## Join the team

If you’re interested in joining OttersAndWeasels, we’d love to hear from you. Tell us which artists you’re into, and send a track/album/ep review to office {at} ottersandweasels (dot) com  with "contributor" in the title.
 
## DJ Sets by ottersandweasels

You can also send your demos to us, and **book us for DJ Sets. **

But we don't play this typical house techno dance stuff and we are not perfect in mixing. We just play a multi genre mix of cool music consisting of new tracks (best of the month) + our all time favorites of the last 50 Years in Music. Usually we are lazy bastards and DJ with a laptop and Traktor software and a mixer (and turntables if available, but for special occasions we dj strictly with VINYL.
